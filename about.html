About Stick Arc

Creating free characters & animations for creators

Stick Arc is a creative platform built for animation lovers, game developers, and students who want high-quality stick-style characters for free.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple — to make animation resources easily available for learning, practice, and creative projects without any cost.

What We Offer

  • Free stick animation characters
  • Character previews and animations
  • Resources for students and beginners
  • Creative inspiration for game & animation projects

Who Is This For?

Stick Arc is made especially for:

  • Students learning animation or game development
  • Beginner animators
  • Creative hobbyists
  • Content creators

Copyright & Usage

All characters and animations provided on Stick Arc are intended for learning, practice, and creative use. Redistribution or commercial use may require proper credit.

Future Plans

We plan to add more characters, animations, tutorials, and creative tools in the future to support the growing animation community.

Contact

If you have suggestions, feedback, or character requests, feel free to use our request form available on the website.
