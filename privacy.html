Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Welcome to Stick Arc. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information such as name, phone number, or address. However, some non-personal data may be collected automatically.

  • Browser type
  • Device type (mobile or desktop)
  • Pages visited on our website

2. Cookies

Stick Arc may use cookies to improve user experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device and do not contain personal information.

3. Google AdSense

In the future, we may display ads provided by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) to show ads based on user visits.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites such as YouTube or Discord. We are not responsible for the privacy policies of those websites.

5. Children’s Information

Stick Arc does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the request form on our website.
